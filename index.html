How to Use?

IcoLoom provides high-quality custom icons to enhance your desktop experience.

  • 1. Download a Custom Icon: Choose and download any icon (ICO file) from the collection.
  • 2. Right-Click on Folder: Select the folder you want to customize, then click on Properties.
  • 3. Go to Customize Tab: Inside the properties, navigate to the Customize tab.
  • 4. Click on Change Icon: Press the Change Icon button, then click Browse.
  • 5. Apply and Enjoy: Choose your downloaded icon file and click Apply & OK.
